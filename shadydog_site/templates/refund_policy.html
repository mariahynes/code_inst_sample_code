Return and Refund Policy

Last updated: February 24, 2021

Thank you for shopping at Shady Dog Design.

We offer a 14-day return policy; the 14 days start from the day on which you receive your order.

Please ensure to use sturdy packaging when returning any item.
Do not send us returns without first getting an email authorisation from Shady Dog Design.
Please enclose a copy of the email in your return, as we cannot accept returns we know nothing about.

When returning an item please use recorded delivery, as we can not process refunds or replacements for items we never receive.

If you wish to return anything we must receive it within 7 days of our return authorisation email being issued.
Upon satisfactory receipt we will refund your credit or debit card the relevant amount less shipping.

In the unlikely event of receiving a damaged product(s), please notify us via email within 7 days to support@shadydogdesign.com
